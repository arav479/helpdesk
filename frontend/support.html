🎫 How to Raise a Support Ticket

Follow these 5 steps to submit your issue
Step Action Details
1 Go to Dashboard Click "Open New Ticket" from the sidebar on the left.
2 Select a Help Topic Choose the category that matches your issue — e.g. Wi-Fi, AC Problem, Plumbing, Attendance.
3 Fill in Ticket Details Enter a brief summary, detailed explanation, your location, department, and mobile number.
4 Submit the Ticket Click "Create Ticket". You will receive a confirmation with a unique Ticket ID.
5 Track Your Ticket Go to "My Tickets" from the sidebar to see status updates and assigned engineer details.
