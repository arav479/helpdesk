Welcome to Helpdesk

Please use this portal to log an incident for any issues you face or observe. Our dedicated support team covers AC Systems, Internet / Wi-Fi, Electrical, Plumbing, Attendance Systems, and more.

Main Portal:
https://helpdesk.ssn.edu.in

Login using Digital ID Credentials (same as Active Directory/LMS/ERP)

Password Recovery:
https://passwordreset.microsoftonline.com/

Steps for Incident Logging

  • Click the "Open New Ticket" button
  • Sign in using your Digital ID credentials
  • Select the appropriate Help Topic
  • Fill in all the mandatory fields
  • Click "Create Ticket" to submit

Checking Status: To check the status/updates to your tickets, simply sign in and navigate to the "My Tickets" section in your dashboard.
